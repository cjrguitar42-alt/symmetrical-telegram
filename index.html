Chris Richter

Chris Richter is a classical guitarist from Dallas, Texas. With a unique repertoire of music spanning traditional classical pieces to modern popular music, he has performed throughout Texas at venues everywhere from house shows to Dallas's legendary House of Blues. In addition to that he has also released several albums of music that have garnered over 20,000,000 streams on Spotify.
